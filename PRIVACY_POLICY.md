#Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Mizuhara respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Bot handles data.
1. Data Collection
The Bot does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.
The Bot may process Discord interactions (such as slash commands) only for execution purposes.
No messages, content, or user activity is permanently saved.
2. Third-Party Services
The Bot operates within Discord and follows Discord’s Privacy Policy: https://discord.com/privacy⁠�
No third-party analytics, tracking, or data-selling services are used.
3. Data Security
Since the Bot does not store user data, there is no risk of long-term data exposure.
All interaction data is processed temporarily and discarded immediately after execution.
4. Server Usage
The Bot may create, modify, or manage server content only when authorized by server permissions and user commands.
The Bot does not access or modify anything outside of Discord’s permitted API scope.
5. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.
Continued use of the Bot after updates means you accept the revised policy.
